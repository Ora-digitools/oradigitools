What Does the Board Do?

Board Role and Responsibilities

The first qualification is to be working in the ECA Role. To be successful in certification you must be in the position to lead and execute ECAL engagements. Are you assigned ECA in an NATD ECA LOB org as follows:

  • Enterprise ECA (Robin Link)
  • National ECA (Edwin Upson)
  • Public Sector ECA (Aaron Cornfeld)
  • Retail & HC ECA (Alan Levine)
  • SSI ECA (Larry Welles)

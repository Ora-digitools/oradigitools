In general, to engage our Hub SEs, Sales Reps should make SE service and resource requests through SCRM. See the request process below for LOB details and exceptions. Note that CPRs should make requests via their ECA contacts.

All NATD SEs (i.e. ECAs, CSMs, Business Value Consultants, Industry Advisors, Platform Specialists and Hub SEs) are assigned to SE Services according to their role and skills. In particular, Platform Specialists and/or Hub SEs are assigned to services based on several criteria, starting with their skillset and specialty technology pillars. In general, Platform Specialists are assigned when on-site customer contact is needed. Hub SE resources are assigned based on workload and scaling needs, time constraints (e.g. need for 24x7 development) and special restrictions such as the need for US-only based resources.

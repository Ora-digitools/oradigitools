Mission

To engineer (at high-scale and high-velocity) innovative customer and industry Cloud solutions in support of the Oracle Sales lifecycle in order to drive more pipeline, close more deals, and foster successful customer Cloud adoption.

Strategy

To create specially designed hub facilities, staffed by full-stack Cloud developers and solution engineers, which foster an environment where real innovation can occur at rapid speed.

Our Focus/Core Competency

The Cloud Hub SEs are especially focused on rapidly •developing full-stack industry and customer Cloud POVs using cutting-edge Oracle technologies with the intention of “wowing” our customers. Think mobile everything, industry-focused IOT/big data analytic solutions, AI chatbots and even solutions using augmented and virtual reality. The idea is to demo solutions that customers will want in their immediate future.

Our Services

The Solution Engineers in our Cloud Hubs combine forces with our field solution engineers, ECAs and CSMs in order to deliver against our complete set of services (as found in the SEaaS Catalog). These services provide critical solution engineering support throughout the Oracle Sales lifecycle:

  • from building Awareness via Technical Workshops,
  • to driving Demand via innovative Solution Demos and delivery of a Cloud Value Hypothesis,
  • to executing on Opportunities via sizing workloads and developing POVs,
  • to accelerating customer cloud adoption and Success via performing Health,
  • Checks and much, much more.
